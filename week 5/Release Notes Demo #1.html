Hardware

1. Make the circuit as shown in "OneShiftRegisterWiringDiagram"

2. The shift register has 8 outputs. Wire each output to a LED that is protected by a resistor.

  1. QA > resistor > LED #1
  2. QB > wire > resistor > LED #2
  3. QC > wire > resistor > LED #3
  4. QD > wire > resistor > LED #4
  5. QE > wire > resistor > LED #5
  6. QF > wire > resistor > LED #6
  7. QG > wire > resistor > LED #7
  8. QH > wire > resistor > LED #8

Software

The code listing on Slide 19 of Lecture 5 toggles the first LED.

The code listing on Slide 16 of Lecture 5 shows you how to loop for 8-bits, which equals 1 byte.
