Hardware

1. Continue from Demo #2

2. Remove the 16 x LEDs

3. To wire a shift register to a 7-segment display:

  1. QA > wire > resistor > wire > segment A
  2. QB > wire > resistor > wire > segment B
  3. QC > wire > resistor > wire > segment C
  4. QD > wire > resistor > wire > segment D
  5. QE > wire > resistor > wire > segment E
  6. QF > wire > resistor > wire > segment F
  7. QG > wire > resistor > wire > segment G
  8. QH > wire > resistor > wire > DP (decimal point)

Reference: "7 Segment LED Displays Using a Shift Register" in the "External Resources" folder. This resource shows you how to make the circuit for 1 x 7-segment display. Apply what you've learned, and repeat for the second 7-segment display.
