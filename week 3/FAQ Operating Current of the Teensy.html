Q: when calculating the correct size of resistors to use (Task 2), you used 9mA. Where did you get the "9mA"?

A: The operating current of the Teensy is 9mA

You can see for yourself:

Data Sheets > Microcontroller > MK20DX256VLH7 > K20P64M72SF1 > Table 4 > Voh where Vdd is between 2.7 V and 3.6 V (page 13)

Regarding quizzes / exams, the operating current will be provided to you. No need to memorize: 9mA
