Android provides other types of layouts. Here is a short list:

TableLayout

A Layout that arranges its children into rows and columns.

TableLayout is a ViewGroup that displays child View elements in rows and columns.

FrameLayout

A Layout that places its children on top of each other so that the latest child is covering the previous. Think of a deck of cards; just like that.

AbsoluteLayout

A Layout that lets you specify exact locations (x/y coordinates) of its children. Absolute layouts are less flexible and harder to maintain than other types of layouts with absolute positioning.
